Chowmein

Description

Chowmein is a popular stir-fried noodle dish made with wheat noodles, vegetables, and optional meat, flavored with soy sauce and aromatic spices. It is known for its savory, slightly smoky taste and crispy or soft texture, depending on the cooking style.

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • Bell pepper
  • Cabbage
  • Diced meat
